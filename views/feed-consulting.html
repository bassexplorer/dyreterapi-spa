Procedures For Sick Animals

We are not veterinarians and what we do can by no means replace a visit with the vet. If your horse or dog is ill or shows signs that could potentially be related to illness or other medical conditions, always contact your veterinarian first.If your horse or dog is on medication, you must inform it before any consultation as we do not massage or do any other physical balancing on sick animals

Healthy feeding habits

Finding the right feed is important in part to make sure your animal gets all the important nutrients it needs. This applies both when we talk about vitamins and minerals, but the content of proteins, fat and carbohydrates is also a very important piece.

What we give our animals to eat are potential building blocks for the body's cells, organs, immune system, digestive system.

Feeding problems can be expressed by:

  • Fur and skin problems
  • Bad immune system
  • Fordøjelsesproblematikker
  • Adfærdsproblemer
