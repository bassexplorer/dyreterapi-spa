  • Physiotherapeutic training for horses

Some more appropriate movement patterns.

In connection with other Physical therapy, such as Massage and Cranio sacral therapy, we often give the owner physiotherapeutic exercises for the horse, which the equipage must train between we see.

These may include, for example, coordination exercises, stretching, flexibility training, (re) training of certain muscle groups, or so-called "proprioceptory training" on other surfaces.
