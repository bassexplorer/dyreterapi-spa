We offer our help and support for you dog problems.

Occasionally, it can be difficult for dogs to function in our man-made world. We have adapted everyday life to the wishes and needs of people, but it does not necessarily suit the dogs. They have the drives and instincts, they are herd animals, they can get stressed and they communicate completely differently than we do.

Thus, much of the unwanted behavior that we call problem behavior is really natural behavior for dogs, it just does not fit when the dogs have to work with us in a daily life. Other causes include bad experiences - including shock, lack of or inappropriate embossing or socialization.

Examples of reasons that may trigger behavior problem

  • anxiety
  • aggression
  • home problems alone
  • stress
  • hyperactivity
  • general exercise problems
