Theoretical Behavior and
Learning Psychology

Duration : 4 - 6 weekends

Price: 8,800 DKK




What we focus on

In this course, we start off with all the basic concepts that come into
play quickly and create a lot of debate and confusion when we talk
about what horses are really like. The theory will be supported by
images and video clips and plenty of examples.

The main subjects covered

Behavioral biology and horse behavior +written assignment.
Learning Psychology 1
Learning Psychology 2
Learning Psychology 3 +written assignment.

Theoretical teaching including the following learning objectives:

What is behavior?
Analysis of various body positions
Sound Communication
Pathological behavior
Aggressiveness
