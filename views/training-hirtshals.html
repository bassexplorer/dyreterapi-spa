Training for all dogs

At this location we offer trainings for dogs at all age. Training for puppies and training for all dog owners are held on certain appointments. See our training plans below.

Address

Center for Animal Therapy, Kringelhøjvej 11A, 9850 Hirtshals

Puppies Team Training

Number of meetings: 7 in total

Price: 750 DKK

Max. 8 puppies per team

What we focus on

The purpose of this team is to lay a good foundation for cooperation and understanding between dog and owner. Our excellent dog behaviour instructor makes sure that your puppy learns the proper disciplines and becomes a great partner of yours.

Schedule

6 following meetings

Practical teaching including the following tasks:

  • Socialization / puppy play
  • Sit
  • Tire
  • Yes No
  • Invitation
  • Handling
  • Space
  • Walk nicely on a leash
  • Contact
  • Socialization trip to the city

Training For All

Number of meetings: 6 in total

Price: 695 DKK

Max. 8 dogs per team

What we focus on

On the team there is room for everyone who wants to go for training with their dog. The focus will be on cooperation between the dog and the owner.

Exercises

We offer obedience exercises and solving training problems based on positive training principles. We also work with the exercises go nicely on a leash, call and the good contact.In addition, dedicated time will be set for individual problems, wishes and ideas.
