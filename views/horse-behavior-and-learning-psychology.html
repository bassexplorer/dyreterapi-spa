Learn how to understand your animal

Don't have the desire or opportunity to take a full horse training consultant, but would you like to learn more about horse behavior, how horses learn (learning psychology) as well as hands-on training from the ground up? Then this is the right course for you!
There are two versions of the course of behavior and learning:

Theoretical Behavior and
Learning Psychology

Duration: 4-6 weekends

Price: 8.800 DKK

What we focus on

In this course, we start off with all the basic concepts that come into play quickly and create a lot of debate and confusion when we talk about what horses are really like. The theory will be supported by images and video clips and plenty of examples.

The main subjects covered

  • Behavioral biology and horse behavior + written assignment
  • Learning Psychology 1
  • Learning Psychology 2
  • Learning Psychology 3+ written assignment

Theoretical teaching including the following learning objectives:

  • What is behavior?
  • Analysis of various body positions
  • Sound Communication
  • Pathological behavior
  • Aggressiveness
